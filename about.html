About Radhika Agencies

Authorised Distributor, Trusted Partner

Radhika Agencies is a young, dynamic authorised distributor of quality generic medicines, founded and led by Mr. Mukul Aggarwal and Mr. Sumit Aggarwal.

We ensure uninterrupted supply of genuine, quality-assured medicines to pharmacies, clinics, hospitals and government health institutions — combining deep pharmaceutical-domain expertise with a modern, technology-driven distribution approach.

Our Role

  • Supply genuine, quality-assured generic medicines to pharmacies and healthcare institutions
  • Cater to government bulk tenders and public health programs
  • Maintain temperature-controlled warehousing & logistics
  • Ensure full traceability and batch-wise documentation

Our Commitment

  • 100% genuine medicines from WHO-GMP certified manufacturers
  • Transparent, government-notified pricing — no hidden costs
  • On-time delivery with audit-ready records
  • Responsive customer support
